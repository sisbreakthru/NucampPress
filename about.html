Linda L. Smith

Linda L. Smith has approximately 20 years experience as a ColdFusion web developer. She has a Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering from Purdue University, West Lafayette, IN; a BA in Visual Communication Digital Design from American Intercontinental University Online (AIU); and is currently attending the Nucamp Full Stack Web and Mobile Development coding bootcamp.
