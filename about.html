Our Founders

Linda L. Smith

Linda L. Smith has approximately 20 years experience as a ColdFusion web developer for the Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL), Oak Ridge, TN. She has developed ColdFusion Web-based applications for spill data records, federal register records, emergency response records, radionuclide inventory records, and other regulatory records. She also has experience in Flash, PHP, MySQL, Agora Shopping Cart, CSS, HTML, Java Script, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and Dreamweaver. She has a Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering from Purdue University, West Lafayette, IN; a BA in Visual Communication Digital Design from American Intercontinental University Online (AIU); and is currently attending the Nucamp Full Stack Web and Mobile Development coding bootcamp.
